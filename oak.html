Oak Park

Oak park is on Alpine Ave, Stockton, CA. Oak is filled with nice tall trees and large green areas. This park contains an ice arena which is nice for winter sports and is something that most parks don't have. Also this park has two jungle gyms, a basketball court, a sand area for some beach volleyball, an pool, a baseball field and some tennis courts. The park is always lively with kids and families having fun. Oak park is a landmark in Stockton where you can go and always find something to do.
